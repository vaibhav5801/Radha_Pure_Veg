Our Story

"Serving authentic vegetarian flavors with warmth and tradition."

Radha Pure Veg in Pune is a highly-rated, family-friendly restaurant chain with locations in Wakad, Nigdi, and Vishrantwadi. We serve authentic North Indian, South Indian, and Chinese cuisine.

Known for quick service and exceptional quality dishes, we pride ourselves on creating memorable dining experiences for families and food lovers alike. Our signature dishes like Paneer Makhanwala have won the hearts of thousands.
